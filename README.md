## Lidar Robot

- 1 base
- 4 motors
	- A narduino to control the motors
	- 1 L298N shield
	- to be installed : change the motors with rotation sensors
- 1 lidar
- 3 Ultrasonic sensors
- 1 camera
- 2 servos to move the camera
